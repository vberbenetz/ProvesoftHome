What is Provesoft?

A cloud based application for storing business documentation, controlling company processes and building a culture of continuous improvement within companies.

Why is it different?

Unlike systems of the past, we have focused on the concept of peer collaboration. Provesoft still performs standard document management functions but has a youthful integration of social media concepts that help teams really work together.

Is my information secure?

All Provesoft traffic is encrypted using 256 bit SSL encryption (HTTPS / SSH) and hosted on the worlds more trusted application hosting service. Rest assured we follow the strictest of standards because we know how important your information is.

Is there a workflow engine?

Yes! We have built in a custom workflow engine to allow you the flexability to approve document changes. Workflows are optionally used for companies that want a higher level of control, but are available to all companies with an account.

Can I be notified if there are changes to my documents?

Yes, as soon as you log into Provesoft you will have a list of document changes that have been approved. This can be found on your "News Feed". You can optionally follow a document by adding it to your favorites list. This will send you email notifications when changes to these documents occur.

Can I access work documents while out of the office?

Yes, if your company has chosen to use the cloud based version of Provesoft you will have access from any location that has an internet connection.

How can I suggest improvements on processes?

Navigate to the document search page and simply find the process or document you want to make a suggestion on. When you click on the document the right hand pane will open up for you to make suggestions.

Can others see my suggestions?

Yes, Provesoft is optimized for employee collaboration. For that reason others will be able to see your suggestions and contribute to the discussion.

How can I make sure that I am always using the latest version of a file?

The latest version of a file will always be sorted to the top of the list of documents. You can optionally explore old versions of documents and processes by clicking the past revisions.

How do revisions work?

Every time a change is made the document or process will change its revision to the next alphabetical letter. For example changing revision A will result in Revision B and so forth.

What support is offered with our membership?

With any membership, we are here to support you with any application issues or concerns you have. Training and deployment are included in your membership. Extra services such as on site training can be scheduled, travel costs may be charged seperatly.

Why Provesoft over other providers

Provesoft makes your process management initiatives as easy as possible. We don't make you go through hours of administration setup, we give you all the tools you need as soon as you register. Our "Just add Water" approach allows you and your company to get up and running instantly, with no technical administrator required and no IT involvement.

How does this compare to Sharepoint?

We completely respect what Sharepoint can do, it is a powerful tool when used correctly. However Provesoft truly focuses in on companies that want to manage their process documentation. Provesoft is lightweight, meaning there is no need for technical Sharepoint developers or IT support. Within minutes your department can create a space that makes managing critical business documents a breeze.

Why should I care about managing my processes?

  • Helps keep consistency in your workforce
  • Gives your go-getters a way to learn more about company processes outside of their scope of work
  • You cannot improve how you do things if you don't understand how things are currently done
  • Being able to see processes that are affected by another allows you to perfect change management

What is Provesoft's Mission?

We believe that all companies can have control over their internal processes without spending endless hours or money doing so. Our experience with companies who want to control internal processes showed us that building a culture is the most important step, and having a simple tool to work with is critical. When systems become to complex employees are less likely to be engaged.

The Social Mission

We wanted to tie in social aspects that we've all become used to using in our personal lives. We believe that given the right medium to contribute, employees will work together to improve their day to day activities. Being able to make suggestions and "Like" different actions that happen within the system provides just enough recognition to spur on employees to contribute more.

The Culture Mission

Hands down the most difficult part of implementing any process or software is employee buy-in and change management. Provesoft (Both the software and implementation services) aim to start a culture within our client's companies of wanting to contribute to the advancement of the company. Culture starts from the top down, so when implementing Provesoft we strongly recommend executive management buy-in and enthusiasm. Contribution stats are stored on each employee profile and serve as a indicator of how engaged employees are willing to get in improving internal processes. Contribution stats should be praised!

Designed For the User

Every element of Provesoft was developed for optimal user experience. We know how important simplicity is when it comes to business software. When you first log into Provesoft we believe you will feel instantly familiar and comfortable navigating the modules, to find what you are looking for.
